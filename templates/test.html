The Akiba Philosophy

Travel is not a reward. It is a right. Akiba Safaris exists because the best places in Kenya — the ones Kenyans have been told to admire from a distance — belong to us first. Every property was selected to be within financial reach of a household, a Chama group, or an individual saver. We carry your financial wellbeing like we carry the journey: carefully, with both hands.

Lipa Kidogo Kidogo — How It Works

Pay a 20–30% deposit to confirm your booking. Then save monthly in instalments of up to 12 months — automatically tracked. On the 5th of every month, your personalised WhatsApp reminder arrives. Your rate is frozen at booking. Your money is always yours. No penalties for life circumstances. Just a journey, built one payment at a time.

Our cancellation framework is built to protect you first — and us second. Know exactly where you stand the day you book. We will always reschedule before we retain.

The Akiba Cancellation Principle

When you save toward a journey through Lipa Kidogo Kidogo, you build something — a plan, a commitment, an expectation. Akiba's policy is designed around protecting what you've built. We do not charge penalties on genuine emergencies confirmed in writing within 48 hours. We do not retain deposits for force majeure circumstances (government travel advisories, natural disasters, declared national emergencies). We will always reschedule before we retain. For Chama group bookings, the group organiser is the sole contact — individual member withdrawals do not automatically forfeit the group's deposit. The Chama is the client. Akiba stands behind that principle.
