The 2026 Safari Rally: Regenerative Adrenaline Package

4 Days of Dust. 100% Carbon-Neutral. The Future of Motorsport.

Dates: March 11 – 15, 2026

Location:
Naivasha & The Great Rift Valley
Host:
RawUnwind Safaris
Experience:
Regenerative Motorsport Safari

Day 1: The "Zero-Trace" Arrival (March 11)

Arrival: Private, low-emission transfer from JKIA/Nairobi to the Naivasha Service Park.

Experience: VIP evening briefing at your lakeside lodge. Meet our Carbon Impact Specialist who will walk you through the math of how your trip is already being offset.

The Mission: 40% of your carbon levy is today released to the Naivasha Wetland Protection project.

Day 2: The Dust & The Dawn (March 12)

Morning: Official Shakedown at Loldia. Witness the WRC1 hybrid cars testing the "fesh-fesh" sand.

Afternoon: Ceremonial Start. RawUnwind guests enjoy a private viewing deck away from the crowds.

The Impact: For every liter of fuel used by our 4x4s today, we plant 5 mangroves in Gazi Bay via Mikoko Pamoja.

Day 3: The Hard Yards – Kedong & Geothermal (March 13)

Action: The longest day of the rally. We take you to the famous "Miti Mbili" jump at Kedong. Feel the earth shake as the world’s best drivers push 500hp through the volcanic dust.

Lunch: Gourmet bush picnic featuring 100% locally sourced, plastic-free ingredients.

The Impact: Your "Adrenaline Pass" funds the Wildlife Works Kasigau Corridor, protecting the elephants that cross these very rally routes.

Day 4: The Sleeping Warrior (March 14)

Action: Deep into the Soysambu Conservancy. Technical stages around Sleeping Warrior and Elmenteita.

Exclusive Access: Visit the Service Park for a behind-the-scenes look at how teams like Toyota and Hyundai are using 100% Sustainable Fuels.

The Impact: RawUnwind sponsors a "Clean-Up Crew" that follows our spectator group to ensure the Rift Valley is cleaner than we found it.

Day 5: The Power Stage & The Legacy (March 15)

Finale: The Wolf Power Stage at Hell’s Gate. Watch the final sprint against the backdrop of towering obsidian cliffs.

Celebration: Podium ceremony in Naivasha.

Departure: Return to Nairobi. You receive your WRC 2026 Carbon Retirement Certificate—a digital asset proving your rally experience was 100% climate-positive.

What’s Included for the "Millionaire" Fan:

  • Transportation: Professional-grade 4x4s with expert drivers.
  • Offsetting: Full CO₂ calculation and retirement via Plan Vivo standards.
  • VIP Access: Prime spectator spots, radio headsets for live commentary, and luxury hampers.
  • Community: A direct donation in your name to the Gazi Bay Education Fund.
