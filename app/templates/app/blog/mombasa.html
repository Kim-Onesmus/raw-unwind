3-Day "Safari to Sea" Regenerative Shore Excursion

The Experience

RawUnwind Safaris invites your distinguished guests to step off the ship and into the soul of Kenya. Designed for the February 2026 docking of the Crystal Symphony and other luxury vessels, this 3-day/2-night transition offers a seamless blend of world-class wildlife viewing and impactful conservation.

Day 1: VIP Port Arrival & The Elephant Corridor

Private 4x4 transfer from Mombasa Port.

Afternoon game drive in the Kasigau Corridor, home to over 11,000 elephants.

Stay at a Gold-Rated Eco-Lodge (Half Board).

Day 2: Conservation in Action

Sunrise game drive followed by a private session with local rangers.

Experience the raw beauty of human-wildlife coexistence.

Day 3: The Blue Carbon Return

Journey back to the coast with an exclusive stop at the Mikoko Pamoja mangrove project.

See the direct impact of your travel before a VIP return to the Port or Moi International Airport.

Why Partner with RawUnwind Safaris?

We don't just sell seats; we sell a Net-Positive legacy.

  • Verified Carbon Offsets: Every guest's footprint is audited and offset through our partners, Wildlife Works and Mikoko Pamoja.
  • The Certificate of Impact: Guests receive a personalized, high-quality Sustainability Certificate validating their contribution to Kenya’s rangelands.
  • VIP Logistics: Dedicated 4x4 Land Cruisers, expert conservationist guides, and 24/7 concierge support for cruise schedule synchronization.
