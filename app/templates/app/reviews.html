The Great Migration · Maasai Mara
★★★★★
"

We have been fortunate to travel extensively — Antarctica, Patagonia, the Maldives before the crowds found them. Nothing — and I mean nothing — prepared us for standing at the Mara River at dawn, watching one million wildebeest decide, collectively and without signal, to cross. Our guide James sat with us in silence after. He didn't rush it. He said one word in Maasai — nkanyit — a deep reverence for the life of another. I have been thinking about that word every day since we returned. That is not a tour. That is an education in how to be alive. Raw Unwind delivered something we did not know was possible: a journey that made us feel small in the best way imaginable.

Lamu Cultural Festival · 7 Days
★★★★★
"

I've stayed at Singita. I've done the Serengeti. I thought I had done Africa. Then I walked through Lamu Old Town at 5 AM, before the island woke, with a historian who has spent 30 years living inside its walls. He showed me a door carved in 1432. He told me the pattern on it records the family's lineage and their trading route to Arabia — in a language most people walk past without reading. I stood there for twenty minutes. That kind of stillness is what I travel for. Raw Unwind understood that without me having to explain it.
