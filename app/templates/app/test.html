"I didn't start a safari company. I started a proof — that a Kenyan operator, rooted in the land, can out-think, out-feel, and out-last anyone else in this market."

Eric Koome · Founder & Director

Eric Koome was raised in the shadow of Mount Kenya in Nyeri, with ancestral roots deep in Makueni County — Akamba land. Before founding Raw Unwind, he spent years in financial services, learning how capital moves and how institutions serve — and fail — their communities.

That background lives in every pricing decision, every partnership clause, and every instalment plan designed to let a Kenyan teacher afford the same wilderness their ancestors walked. Raw Unwind is not managed from a city desk. It is managed from inside the story.

How Raw Unwind is structured

Raw Unwind Safaris is the licensed parent brand. We serve two distinct markets through two fluent registers — not two companies, not two missions, but two expressions of the same underlying conviction.

I

Ultra-luxury · USD-priced · International & Kenyan UHNW

Sovereign Safari

Private itineraries for guests who have seen the world and want to understand Africa. Maximum 8 guests per journey. Every detail considered. The Passage — our flagship seven-night digital detox wilderness immersion — is the purest expression of this register.

  • Priced from USD 7,900 per person
  • Private conservancy access
  • Carbon-neutral by design
  • International DMC network (UK, Turkey, Spain, Vietnam)
  • Maximum 8 guests
II

Domestic sub-brand · KES-priced · Wanachi market

Akiba Safaris

Safari ni nyumbani. Built for the Kenyan middle class — teachers, chamas, young professionals — through instalment plans, WhatsApp group booking, and experiences designed to feel like a homecoming, not a performance of wealth.

  • 1–12 month instalment model
  • Chama economy distribution
  • Self-drive coast packages
  • M-Pesa integrated booking
  • KES-priced, rack × 1.20–1.30
